## Status Legend

- **Not started** — Section identified but no drafting begun
- **In progress** — Drafting underway
- **Drafted** — Section drafted but not reviewed
- **Reviewed** — Section drafted and reviewed (Red Team)
- **Final** — Section final and ready for submission

Update the Status column as drafting progresses. The matrix becomes the single source of truth for proposal completion.
